## Summary

The streaming search endpoint (`/api/search/stream`) in `internal/server/server.go` sends results as they're discovered during filesystem walks, causing them to arrive in filesystem order rather than fzf relevance order. The frontend attempts to re-sort, but this creates a poor UX where results jump around.
